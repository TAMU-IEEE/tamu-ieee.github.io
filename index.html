TAMUMake Committee: Available Positions

Thank you for your interest in serving as a committee leader for tamuMAKE Hackathon! We look forward to the opportunity to work with you.

Have a look at the job descriptions & what we are looking for in this IEEE committee.

Skills listed are beneficial, but not required.

Tech Corporate Sponsorship Chair

Responsibilities

  • Acts as liaison between tech corporations and Hackathon team
  • Recruit corporate sponsorship from tech companies
  • Coordinate corporate presentations during Hackathon
  • Organize judges from corporate sponsors

Skills

  • Interpersonal/communication

Local Corporate Sponsorship Chair

Responsibilities

  • Acts as liaison between local companies and Hackathon team
  • Recruit local company sponsorships (CC Creations, Kolache Rolf’s, Starbucks, etc.)
  • Coordinate corporate presentations during Hackathon
  • Coordinate donations for dining, prizes, and “swag”

Skills

  • Interpersonal/communication

Marketing Chair

Responsibilities

  • Design Hackathon logo
  • Design Hackathon “swag” (t-shirts, lanyards, etc.)
  • Aid in Hackathon website design and maintenance
  • Create and post advertisements for Hackathon

Skills

  • Design software (Photoshop, CorelDraw, etc.)

Apply for the committee!

Applications should be emailed along with a copy of your resume to the listed email. Interviews will be scheduled soon after your application is received. Decisions will be made after interviews are completed. If you have questions regarding the application or responsibilities, do not hesitate to contact through email.

The committee leadership position is external to the IEEE officer team. Participation in IEEE is not necessary, but favorable!
